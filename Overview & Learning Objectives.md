# Active Directory Administration Lab Guide

## Overview

This comprehensive lab guide provides hands-on training for **Active Directory (AD)** administrators across three proficiency levels.

Throughout this guide, you will work with:

* Group Policy Object (GPO) operations
* DNS management
* Domain controller maintenance
* Delegation
* Troubleshooting in production-like environments

The lab is structured into three progressive proficiency levels, with each level building upon the previous one—from guided tasks to advanced challenge scenarios.

> [!NOTE]
> This lab is designed to simulate real-world enterprise Active Directory administration scenarios, allowing participants to develop practical operational skills in a controlled environment.

---

## Learning Objectives

Upon completing this lab, you will be able to:

* ✅ Configure and manage **Group Policy Objects (GPOs)** at scale.
* ✅ Perform **DNS management** and troubleshooting.
* ✅ Maintain **domain controller replication** and overall health.
* ✅ Implement **delegation** and permission models.
* ✅ Monitor **Active Directory services** and respond to operational incidents.
* ✅ Execute **patch management** and system upgrades.
* ✅ Conduct **health checks** and compliance validation.

> [!TIP]
> Complete the learning objectives in sequence, as later exercises assume knowledge and configurations established in earlier sections.
